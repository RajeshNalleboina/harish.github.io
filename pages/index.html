Child & Community Development

JRDS so far released more than 2500 laboured, street wandered, destitute, out school children, semi orphan and orphan children and rehabilitated them through our NCLP STCs, NRBCs, RBCs, Seasonal Hostels and URS etc. Educating them corresponding to their age with basic literacy and lessons, we mainstreamed them for formal schooling to Govt schools and got joined them to Govt hostels. Women empowerment is one of the main founding objectives of JRDS. Towards the same JRDS initiates a variety of innovative activities and projects since its inception in 2000. Skilling & Enterprising, Formation of CBOs & Capacity building, Provision of Credits and Thrifts, Literacy Classes, Financial Literacy, Knowledge/Information Dissemination, Networking, etc. are some of the main activities that JRDS so far carried out.
